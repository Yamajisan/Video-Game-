# Eldor's Chronicles / Echoes of Eldrador - Deck Analysis

Source analyzed: `C:\Users\waelk\Downloads\Eldor's Chronicles (1).pdf`

## Naming Decision

The pitch deck uses `Eidryss` for the protagonist. Per project direction, the demo renames the hero to `Aedrys` everywhere in UI, dialogue, quest text, and code-facing lore.

## Lore

- Eldor is the continent; Eldrador is the central realm of ancient mysteries, contrasting regions, elemental traditions, and hidden knowledge.
- The major myth arc is the Star-Dragon Prophecy, tied to relics such as the Aurora Shard, Echo of Elyria, and Cinderheart Amulet.
- Ancient knowledge is preserved in astrology, glyphs, runic wisdom, elven tree-language, dwarven craft, and dragon-linked artifacts.
- The Echo of Elyria is a powerful elven relic hidden in the Emerald Dominion.

## Main Characters

- Aedrys: Player hero, formerly named Eidryss in the deck. A knowledge-seeking traveler whose growth is mirrored by unlocked abilities.
- Eryndor the Wayfarer: Mentor and lore guide. Introduces Eldrador, guides Aedrys toward Dragon's Spine, Arkon Keep, and the Echo of Elyria prophecy.
- King Althaeon and Queen Lythariel: Elven rulers and custodians of Heartwood/El'goroth knowledge.
- Ograan and Ranaar: Dwarven lorekeeper/smith archetypes tied to Highforge and Arkon secrets.
- Ethereal Warden: Silent spectral guardian of forbidden elven lore, used as the vertical slice boss.

## Regions Integrated

- Willowhaven Crossroads: Central hub of commerce, learning, docks, library, and the starting point.
- Ancient Forest Path: Misty conifer route toward the northern mountains and bandit ambush.
- Dragon's Spine Foothills: Rugged, gray, cold mountain terrain with climbable rocks and glacial lore.
- Arkon Keep: Ancient fortress/library, rune door, astral alignment puzzle, Frost Ward reward.
- Emerald Dominion: Luminous green forest, crystal spires, elven magic, Whisperwind reveal.
- Hidden Temple / Veilgrove Arena: Compact boss arena for the Ethereal Warden and Echo of Elyria.

## Story Beats Integrated

1. Aedrys starts at Willowhaven.
2. Aedrys meets Eryndor the Wayfarer.
3. Eryndor explains Eldrador's regions and the Echo of Elyria prophecy.
4. Aedrys travels through the forest path and Dragon's Spine foothills.
5. Aedrys collects ancient glyph fragments.
6. Glyphs open the rune door at Arkon Keep.
7. Aedrys solves an Astral Alignment puzzle.
8. Frost Ward is unlocked.
9. Aedrys reaches the Emerald Dominion.
10. Whisperwind reveals the hidden temple path.
11. Aedrys fights the Ethereal Warden.
12. Aedrys claims the Echo of Elyria.
13. The demo ends with "To Be Continued: The Star-Dragon Prophecy Awakens."

## Gameplay Mechanics Integrated

- Third-person movement and mouse camera.
- Sprint, jump, dash, climbing, gliding, and Windwalk traversal.
- Staff/sword-style melee and arcane projectile.
- Frost Ward defensive spell.
- Whisperwind reveal ability.
- Health, stamina, mana.
- Quest journal, dialogue UI, interaction prompts, boss health bar, victory screen.
- Enemy patrol/chase/attack AI.
- Rune door, glyph collection, astral alignment puzzle, lore tablets, hidden path reveal.

## Art Direction Integrated

- Willowhaven: warmer stone, docks, civic hub silhouettes.
- Dragon's Spine: gray mountain palette, cliffs, rocks, cold fog.
- Arkon Keep: ruined stone, scholar fortress forms, pale arcane lighting.
- Emerald Dominion: luminous greens, crystals, vine-like forest density, ethereal atmosphere.
- Boss arena: crystal ring, spectral cyan-green Warden, cinematic intro.
